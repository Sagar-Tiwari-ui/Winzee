How to Use

  1. Spray directly onto glass surface
  2. Wipe with a microfiber cloth
  3. For best results, use in a circular motion
  4. Buff dry for a streak-free shine

Pro Tips

  • Clean glass on cloudy days to prevent streaking
  • Use newspaper for an extra sparkle
  • Clean from top to bottom
  • Don't forget window tracks and frames

Safety First

  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Use in well-ventilated areas
  • Store in a cool, dry place
